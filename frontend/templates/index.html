About Guardian LLM

Project Overview

Guardian LLM is an advanced AI-powered system designed to automatically assess ethical risks in AI research papers. Using state-of-the-art language models with domain-specific LoRA adapters, it provides comprehensive ethical risk assessment across six critical categories.
