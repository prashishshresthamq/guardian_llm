About Guardian LLM

Project Overview

Guardian LLM is an advanced AI-powered system designed to automatically assess ethical risks in AI research papers. Using a combination of keyword analysis, specialized risk detectors, and transformer-based models, Guardian LLM provides comprehensive ethical risk assessment across six critical categories.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Model Analysis: Combines keyword, specialized, and transformer-based analyzers
  • Six Risk Categories: Comprehensive coverage of ethical concerns
  • Real-time Analysis: Results in under 30 seconds
  • Actionable Recommendations: Specific guidance for risk mitigation

Risk Categories

Bias & Fairness

Detects algorithmic bias, demographic discrimination, and fairness issues in AI systems.

Privacy & Data

Identifies privacy violations, data protection issues, and surveillance concerns.

Safety & Security

Assesses system failures, security vulnerabilities, and adversarial attack risks.

Dual-Use Potential

Evaluates military applications, weaponization potential, and surveillance misuse.

Societal Impact

Analyzes economic displacement, democratic effects, and social consequences.

Transparency

Checks for black box systems, algorithmic accountability, and interpretability issues.
